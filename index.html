The Question

This isn't a debate.
It's a civic reckoning.

AI is already transforming work, healthcare, finance, and governance. Not in theory—in practice. But the conversation about what this means for society is fragmented, polarized, and stuck between hype and fear.

In 60 minutes, 25 people from different sectors will grapple with:

  • → What does AI readiness actually look like for a society?
  • → Who is being left out of the decisions that shape everyone?
  • → What should institutions do now—before the gap widens?

“You can't regulate what you don't understand. And you can't understand what you haven't discussed—together.”
